Giving

If you owuld like to donate to ECC, click on the "Give" button here. All money goes to church operation and mission work. There are no paid positions at ECC. We are all volunteers working to spread the gospel because we firmly believe that this message will change lives.

We freely post financial statements for anyone to look over to see where our money comes from and where it goes. Some of this info is generalized to protect the anonymity of donors and recipients.
